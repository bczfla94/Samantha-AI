# CLAUDE.md

## About Me
I help women transform their bodies and become the most confident version of themselves through online fitness coaching. I've been doing this for 10+ years and have coached thousands of women to lose weight, build strength, and become what I call "unrecognizable" — not just physically, but mentally. Alongside that, I mentor fitness coaches on how to build profitable online coaching businesses and scale to consistent $10k months. I'm also building a software platform called Next Level Coach AI, which provides AI tools for course creators and online coaches to help them save time, improve client retention, manage emails, generate content ideas, and scale without burning out.

## My Audience
Two core audiences:

**Women seeking transformation:** Women who feel like they've tried everything — diets, workouts, programs — but nothing has stuck long term. They're busy with careers, families, demanding lives. They don't want extreme diets or quick fixes. They want a sustainable system to lose weight, build strength, and feel confident again. They want the full identity shift — to become the woman who is disciplined, consistent, and unrecognizable compared to who she used to be.

**Fitness coaches:** Passionate coaches who struggle with getting clients consistently, creating content that attracts the right audience, retaining clients, and building a business that doesn't burn them out. They want consistent $10k months, to become better leaders for their clients, and to build a coaching business that lasts.

## My Voice & Style
Direct, empowering, and motivating — but also supportive and real. I speak like a confident coach who genuinely cares about helping people step into the next level of who they're capable of becoming. Honest and straightforward when someone needs to hear the truth. I focus heavily on identity shifts, not just surface-level tactics. I use the concept of becoming "unrecognizable" — where habits, mindset, and identity completely shift. I want people to feel seen, understood, and capable of more. Inspired, but also leaving with clear direction and belief in themselves. When speaking to coaches, my tone is slightly more strategic and leadership-focused.
