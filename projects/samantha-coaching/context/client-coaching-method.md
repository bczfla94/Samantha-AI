# Samantha Coaching — Client Coaching Method

## Coaching Philosophy
Built around three pillars:
1. Sustainable nutrition
2. Consistent training
3. Lifestyle habits that support long-term results

Goal is not temporary weight loss.
Goal is permanent lifestyle transformation.

---

## Client Transformation Phases

### Phase 1 — Foundation
- Establishing basic habits
- Improving nutrition awareness
- Creating consistency with workouts
- Goal: build momentum

### Phase 2 — Structure
- Structured nutrition
- Consistent training
- Stronger accountability
- Clients begin seeing noticeable results

### Phase 3 — Identity Shift
- Reinforcing identity of a disciplined person
- Long-term lifestyle habits
- Maintaining progress
- Client no longer feels like they are "trying" — they have become a healthier person

---

## Nutrition Approach
- Adequate protein
- Balanced macronutrients
- Sustainable calorie targets
- Flexibility with food choices
- Clients should not feel deprived
- Learn to make smart decisions consistently

---

## Training Approach
- Consistency over perfection
- Progressive strength development
- Manageable workout schedules
- Program must fit real life

---

## Habit Development
- Regular workouts
- Consistent meal planning
- Adequate sleep
- Hydration
- Stress management

---

## Accountability System
Clients expected to:
- Follow their program
- Communicate honestly
- Stay engaged with coaching process

Samantha provides:
- Encouragement
- Honest feedback
- Corrections when necessary

---

## Handling Common Struggles
- Consistency issues → refocus on habits and routines
- Emotional eating → identify triggers, build systems
- Loss of motivation → remind them systems create results not motivation

---

## Handling Plateaus
Evaluate:
- Adherence to program
- Nutrition consistency
- Training intensity
- Sleep and stress

Make adjustments gradually, not dramatically.
