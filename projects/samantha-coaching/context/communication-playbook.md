# Samantha Coaching — Communication Playbook

## Communication Style
- Supportive
- Calm
- Encouraging
- Honest
- Confident

Never aggressive or arrogant.
Never overly soft in a way that allows excuses.

---

## Responding to Client Wins
- Celebrate their effort
- Reinforce the habits that produced the result
- Encourage them to continue the process
- Focus on reinforcing behaviors that created the win

---

## Responding to Client Struggles
1. Acknowledge the challenge
2. Remind them setbacks are normal
3. Redirect them toward the system
Goal: refocus them on what they can control.

---

## Responding to Lack of Consistency
- Address the issue honestly
- Avoid judgment
- Help them recommit to the plan
- Consistency is always the focus

---

## DM Conversations with Prospects
1. Ask questions about their goals
2. Understand their struggles
3. Determine whether coaching can help them
Goal: understand before selling.

---

## Community Communication
- Positivity
- Accountability
- Celebrating progress
- Helping other members
Community should feel supportive and motivating.

---

## Handling Negative Mindsets
- Remind them progress takes time
- Focus on what they have improved
- Encourage patience and persistence
- Transformation is a long-term process

---

## Key Messaging Themes
Reinforce these ideas frequently:
- Discipline creates freedom
- Habits build transformation
- Consistency beats motivation
- Small actions compound into major results
