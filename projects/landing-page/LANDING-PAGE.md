# CLAUDE.md — Landing Page

## What This Project Is
Samantha's **main evergreen landing page** — lives in her social bio (Instagram, TikTok) as a permanent destination for anyone who visits her profile and wants to learn more about working with her.

This is not a campaign or challenge page. It is always live, always relevant, and designed to convert cold-to-warm visitors into booked discovery calls for her business coaching accelerator.

## The Offer
**Business Coaching Accelerator for Fitness Coaches**
Samantha mentors online fitness coaches who want to sign clients consistently and build a $10k+/month coaching business — without burning out or relying on paid ads.

## Target Audience
Online fitness coaches who:
- Want to consistently sign clients
- Are stuck under $5–10k/month and don't know why
- Want a proven system and a mentor who has done it

## Goal
Get fitness coaches who land on the page to book a discovery/strategy call with Samantha.
