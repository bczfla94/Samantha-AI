# CLAUDE.md — Landing Page

## What This Project Is
A landing page for Samantha's 7-day challenge offer targeted at online fitness coaches.

## The Offer
"Book 3+ Sales Calls in 7 Days Without Ads"
The Client Attraction System — a 7-day sprint for online fitness coaches.

## Target Audience
Online fitness coaches who:
- Struggle to sign clients consistently
- Want to reach $10k/month
- Want results without paid ads

## Goal
Get coaches to opt in to the 7-day challenge.
