# Next Level Coach AI — Onboarding and Customer Success

## Initial Setup
When a coach begins using the platform, first step is connecting relevant systems and providing context for AI agents.

This includes:
- Connecting coach's email provider
- Uploading course or program materials
- Providing information about the coaching offer
- Giving AI agents context about the business

More context = more accurate agent outputs.

---

## Agent Familiarization
Coach should become familiar with all five core agents:
- Email Agent
- Retention Agent
- Lead Follow-Up Agent
- Content Creation Agent
- Coach Replica Agent

Understanding each agent's role helps integrate the platform into daily operations.

---

## Daily Workflow
Typical daily usage includes:
- Reviewing drafted email responses
- Checking engagement signals from clients
- Reviewing follow-up prompts for leads
- Generating content ideas for upcoming posts
- Assisting with responses to client questions

---

## Best Practices for Success
Coaches who get best results:
- Consistently review AI-generated suggestions
- Integrate agents into their daily workflow
- Maintain clear communication with clients
- Continue producing content regularly

Platform works best as an operational support system, not a replacement for coach involvement.

---

## Long-Term Success
Over time the platform becomes a central part of the coach's operational workflow. Consistently using AI agents reduces repetitive tasks and creates a more scalable business structure.
