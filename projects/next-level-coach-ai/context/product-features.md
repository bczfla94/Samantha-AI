# Next Level Coach AI — Product Features

## Platform Structure
Specialized AI agents supporting different operational areas of a coaching business.

---

## Email Management
- Reading incoming emails
- Analyzing message context
- Generating drafted responses
- Coach reviews before sending
- Reduces time spent replying manually
NOTE: Email Agent is a drafting assistant, not an autonomous sender.

---

## Client Retention Support
- Identifying possible disengagement
- Surfacing clients who may need support
- Prompting encouraging communication
- Reducing silent churn
- Supporting stronger client relationships

---

## Lead Follow-Up
- Identifying leads who stopped responding
- Generating follow-up communication
- Preventing prospects from slipping away
- Maintaining consistency in lead communication
- Re-engaging ghosted leads

---

## Content Guidance
- Analyzing coach's previous content
- Identifying useful content patterns
- Generating ideas for future posts
- Helping coach decide what to post next
- Supporting consistency in content creation

---

## Coach Knowledge Replication
- Analyzing course materials
- Using program content as reference
- Helping answer repeated client questions
- Reinforcing coach's frameworks and teachings
- Supporting faster responses to common questions

---

## Business Context Integration
Agents operate using business-specific context:
- Coach's offer
- Program materials
- Messaging style
- Educational content
- Communication patterns

---

## Coach Review and Control
- Drafted responses require approval before sending
- Coach's teachings used as source material
- Coach assists rather than fully replaces human judgment

---

## Product Feature Philosophy
Only add features that support a real coaching-business workflow. Practical, easy to understand, operationally useful, aligned with coach needs.
