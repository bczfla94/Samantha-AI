# Next Level Coach AI — Target Market

## Primary Customer
Online coaches who sell coaching programs, courses, or memberships including:
- Fitness coaches
- Business coaches
- Life coaches
- Health coaches
- Mindset coaches
- Course creators with coaching elements

---

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Already selling coaching online
- Actively communicating with clients and leads
- Wants to grow their business
- Experiencing operational pressure as they grow

---

## Core Problems They Experience
- Communication overload
- Repeating the same answers
- Inconsistent follow-up
- Client disengagement
- Content pressure

---

## Language Coaches Use
- "I'm drowning in messages."
- "I answer the same questions every day."
- "I know I should follow up with leads but I forget."
- "Clients just disappear sometimes."
- "I don't know what to post."
- "I spend too much time in my inbox."

---

## Buying Triggers
- Feeling overwhelmed with communication
- Spending too much time replying to messages
- Losing leads due to poor follow-up
- Noticing clients disengaging
- Struggling to keep up with content

---

## Poor Fit Customers
- Coaches with no clients yet
- Coaches not actively running their business
- Coaches who expect AI to fully replace them
