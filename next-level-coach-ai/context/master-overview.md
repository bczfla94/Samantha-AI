# Next Level Coach AI — Master Overview

## What This Company Is

Next Level Coach AI is a specialized AI software platform built specifically for online coaches.

The platform provides purpose-built AI agents that handle critical parts of a coaching business such as:

- email communication
- client retention support
- lead follow-up
- content strategy assistance
- answering client questions based on the coach's knowledge

The goal of the platform is to give coaches leverage by automating repetitive operational work while still allowing the coach to remain in control of their business and client relationships.

Next Level Coach AI is not a generic AI tool.

It is designed specifically for the workflows, communication patterns, and operational needs of online coaching businesses.

---

# Core Mission

The mission of Next Level Coach AI is to help online coaches scale their businesses without becoming overwhelmed by the operational workload that normally comes with growth.

---

# Core Philosophy

1. Coaches should spend their time coaching, not doing admin work
2. Automation should assist the coach, not replace the coach
3. AI should be purpose-built for coaching businesses
4. Client experience and retention matter more than most coaches realize

---

# Business Model

Monthly subscription model providing access to the platform and its AI agents.

---

# Current Known Limitations

- Agents do not currently auto-answer DMs
- Email Agent does not automatically send emails
- Email Agent requires coach approval before sending
- Email Agent connects to email provider only (not Skool)
