# Next Level Coach AI — Internal Terminology

## Company Name
Next Level Coach AI — always use full name externally.
NLCAI — acceptable internal shorthand only.

---

## Core Product Description
"Specialized AI software platform for online coaches"
Use this to distinguish from generic AI tools or broad business software.

---

## Core Structural Term
AI agents — always use this term for the specialized systems inside the platform.

---

## Agent Names
- Email Agent
- Retention Agent
- Lead Follow-Up Agent
- Content Creation Agent
- Coach Replica Agent

---

## Key Value Terms
- Leverage — helping coaches get more output without doing everything manually
- Operational workload — repetitive business tasks the platform helps reduce
- Repetitive communication — repeated emails, questions, follow-up tasks
- Client engagement — retention and support
- Follow-up consistency — value of the Lead Follow-Up Agent
- Coach control — platform supports coach without removing their control

---

## Terms to Use Carefully
- Automation — useful but frame as supportive and controlled, not hands-off
- AI twin — effective in some marketing contexts but don't overuse
- Scale — always ground in practical meaning (scaling communication, support, operations)

---

## Terms to Avoid
- "Fully autonomous coaching replacement"
- "Run your business without you"
- "AI does everything for you"
- "Revolutionary machine intelligence engine"
- Generic AI buzzwords
- Overly technical machine-learning language
- Anything implying the coach becomes unnecessary

---

## Brand Language Rule
Always stay grounded in:
- Coaching businesses
- Real workflows
- Time savings
- Leverage
- Client experience
- Coach control
- Practical usefulness
