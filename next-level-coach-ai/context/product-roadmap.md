# Next Level Coach AI — Product Roadmap

## Long-Term Vision
Become a core operational layer for online coaching businesses — a centralized system where AI agents assist with key areas of the business.

---

## Expansion of AI Agents
Potential future agents:
- Onboarding automation
- Client progress monitoring
- Community engagement assistance
- Testimonial collection
- Content performance analysis
- Educational content recommendations

---

## Deeper Content Intelligence
Future Content Creation Agent capabilities:
- Identifying high-performing content patterns
- Suggesting topics based on audience engagement
- Identifying gaps in content strategy
- Helping structure content series

---

## Expanded Knowledge Systems
Future Coach Replica Agent capabilities:
- Deeper analysis of course materials
- Understanding coaching frameworks
- Answering more complex client questions
- Helping clients navigate educational content

---

## Communication Expansion
Potential future channels:
- Direct messaging integrations
- Community platform interactions
- Multi-channel messaging systems

---

## Potential Integrations
- Email providers
- Community platforms
- Learning management systems
- Calendar systems
- Payment platforms

---

## Guiding Principle
Only build features that solve real operational problems inside coaching businesses. Every new capability should help coaches save time, reduce repetitive work, improve client experience, and scale without burning out.

## IMPORTANT
Items in this file are FUTURE VISION, not current functionality. Do not present these as existing features.
