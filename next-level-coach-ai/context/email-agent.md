# Next Level Coach AI — Email Agent

## Purpose
Assists coaches with managing email communication by generating drafted responses for coach review.

---

## Core Function
- Reads incoming emails
- Generates suggested reply
- Coach approves, edits, or deletes
- Message only sent after coach approval

---

## Key Behavior
Functions as a DRAFT ASSISTANT, not an autonomous email sender. Always requires coach review before sending.

---

## Current Integration
Connects to coach's email provider only.
No longer integrates with Skool or other community platforms.

---

## Problems It Solves
- Repetitive responses to common questions
- Hours spent in the inbox daily
- Communication fatigue from writing all day

---

## Example Use Cases
- Client emails with common program question → agent drafts response → coach reviews and sends
- Prospect inquires about offer → agent drafts response → coach reviews and sends
- Administrative scheduling questions → agent drafts reply → coach confirms and sends

---

## Value to Coach
Handle email communication more efficiently without sacrificing response quality. Review and approve AI drafts instead of writing every response manually.

---

## Customer Quote
"I used to spend half my day writing the same responses over and over. Now it's like my AI twin just handles it — and says it better than I would."
