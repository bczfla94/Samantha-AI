# Samantha Coaching Playbook

## Transformation Framework — 5 Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Nutrition
- Balanced meals
- Portion awareness
- Flexible eating
- Consistency
- Not restrictive — structured but realistic

### Pillar 2 — Training
- Build strength
- Increase metabolism
- Improve body composition
- Must be realistic and sustainable

### Pillar 3 — Habits
- Daily movement
- Hydration
- Sleep routines
- Meal planning
- Consistency with workouts

### Pillar 4 — Mindset
- Overcome self doubt
- Develop discipline
- Stop relying on motivation
- Build self belief

### Pillar 5 — Accountability
- Weekly check-ins
- Feedback
- Adjustments
- Encouragement

## Becoming Unrecognizable Philosophy
The goal is transforming into a new version of yourself including:
- Physical transformation
- Mental growth
- Lifestyle changes
- Increased confidence

Not just weight loss. A new identity.
