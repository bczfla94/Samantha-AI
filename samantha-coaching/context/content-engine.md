# Samantha Content Engine

## Purpose
Use this file when generating:
- Instagram posts
- Captions
- TikTok scripts
- Reels scripts
- Story ideas
- Authority posts
- Client attraction content
- DM conversation starters

Goal: client attraction and authority positioning.

---

## Core Content Philosophy
Every piece of content must do one of three things:
1. Educate
2. Build Authority
3. Create Relatability

---

## 3 Post Weekly Structure

### Post 1 — Callout Post
Calls out the ideal client and their problem.
Example themes:
- "Why most women can't lose weight even though they try hard"
- "Why fitness coaches struggle to sign clients"
Goal: make the ideal client feel seen and understood.

### Post 2 — Authority Post
Demonstrates expertise.
Examples:
- Coaching insights
- Common mistakes clients make
- Lessons learned from coaching
- Frameworks or systems
Goal: position Samantha as an expert.

### Post 3 — Relatable Post
Builds emotional connection.
Examples:
- Personal stories
- Behind the scenes
- Struggles and lessons
- Honest reflections
Goal: make Samantha feel human and relatable.

---

## Hook Strategy
Hooks should interrupt scrolling, speak directly to target client, highlight a problem, create curiosity.

### Hook Types
- Direct Problem: "The reason you're not losing weight isn't your workouts."
- Truth Bomb: "Most women trying to lose weight are doing this completely wrong."
- Callout: "If you're a fitness coach struggling to sign clients, listen to this."
- Pattern Interrupt: "You don't need more motivation."
- Relatable: "I used to believe this about weight loss too."

---

## Story Structure
1. Situation
2. Struggle
3. Lesson
4. Takeaway

---

## Content That Converts
- Clear problem awareness
- Belief shifts
- Positioning Samantha as the guide

---

## Weak Content to Avoid
- Generic motivation
- Vague advice
- Surface level tips
- Overly common fitness advice

Examples of weak content:
"Stay consistent!" "Drink more water!"

---

## Call to Action Strategy
Keep CTAs simple and natural:
- "Message me if this resonates with you."
- "Comment if you relate to this."
- "Send me a message if you're ready to change."
Goal: start conversations.
